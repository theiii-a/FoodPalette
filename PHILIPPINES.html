PHILIPPINES

Ilocos Region - Bagnet

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg pork belly
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 4 cups water
  • Oil for deep frying

Procedures:

  1. Boil the pork belly in water with salt and pepper until tender.
  2. Let it cool, then refrigerate overnight.
  3. Deep fry the pork belly until crispy.
  4. Serve with bagoong and tomatoes.

Cagayan Valley - Pancit Batil Patung

Ingredients:

  • 500g miki noodles
  • 250g ground pork
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cabbage, shredded
  • 1 egg, poached
  • Soy sauce, salt, and pepper to taste

Procedures:

  1. Sauté garlic and onion, then add ground pork.
  2. Add vegetables and cook until tender.
  3. Add noodles and season with soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
  4. Top with poached egg and serve with a side of soup.

Central Luzon - Sisig

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg pig’s face and ears
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • Calamansi juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedures:

  1. Boil pig’s face and ears until tender, then grill until crispy.
  2. Chop into small pieces.
  3. Sauté garlic and onion, then add chopped meat.
  4. Add soy sauce, vinegar, chili flakes, and calamansi juice.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, then serve on a sizzling plate.

CALABARZON - Bulalo

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg beef shank
  • 1 corn, cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 1 bunch pechay (bok choy)
  • 1 bunch green beans
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedures:

  1. Boil beef shank with onion until tender.
  2. Add corn and cook until tender.
  3. Add vegetables and cook until done.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, then serve hot.

MIMAROPA - Kare-Kare

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg oxtail
  • 1 banana heart, sliced
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 1 bunch string beans
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp annatto powder
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedures:

  1. Boil oxtail until tender.
  2. Add vegetables and cook until tender.
  3. Dissolve peanut butter in water and add to the pot.
  4. Add annatto powder and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve with bagoong

Bicol Region - Bicol Express

Ingredients:

  • 500g pork belly, sliced
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 cup coconut cream
  • 5 pcs chili peppers, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp shrimp paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedures:

  1. Sauté garlic and onion, then add pork belly.
  2. Add shrimp paste and chili peppers.
  3. Pour in coconut milk and simmer until pork is tender.
  4. Add coconut cream and cook until sauce thickens.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, then serve.

Western Visayas - La Paz Batchoy

Ingredients:

  • 500g egg noodles
  • 250g pork liver, sliced
  • 250g pork belly, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 liter pork broth
  • Crushed pork cracklings
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedures:

  1. Sauté garlic and onion, then add pork belly and liver.
  2. Pour in pork broth and bring to a boil.
  3. Add noodles and cook until done.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Top with crushed pork cracklings and serve hot.

Central Visayas - Lechon

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole pig
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 1 bunch lemongrass
  • 1 bunch green onions
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedures:

  1. Clean the pig and season with soy sauce, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  2. Stuff the pig with lemongrass and green onions.
  3. Roast over charcoal until skin is crispy and meat is cooked through.
  4. Serve with liver sauce.

Eastern Visayas - Binagol

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups grated taro
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup condensed milk
  • Banana leaves

Procedures:

  1. Mix grated taro, coconut milk, sugar, and condensed milk.
  2. Wrap the mixture in banana leaves.
  3. Steam for 1 hour.
  4. Let cool before serving.

Zamboanga Peninsula - Curacha

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg spanner crab
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 cup coconut cream
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedures:

  1. Sauté garlic and onion, then add crabs.
  2. Pour in coconut milk and simmer until crabs are cooked.
  3. Add coconut cream and curry powder.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, then serve.

Northern Mindanao - Sinuglaw

Ingredients:

  • 500g pork belly, grilled and sliced
  • 250g fish fillet, cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedures:

  1. Mix fish fillet with vinegar, garlic, and onion.
  2. Let it sit for 30 minutes.
  3. Add grilled pork belly and coconut milk.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, then serve.

Davao Region - Durian Candy

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups durian pulp
  • 1 cup condensed milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch

Procedures:

  1. Mix durian pulp, condensed milk, and sugar.
  2. Cook over low heat until thick.
  3. Add cornstarch and cook until mixture thickens.
  4. Let cool, then shape into candies.

SOCCSKSARGEN - Pastil

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 250g chicken breast, shredded
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • Banana leaves

Procedures:

  1. Sauté garlic and onion, then add shredded chicken.
  2. Add soy sauce and cook until chicken is done.
  3. Place rice on banana leaves, top with chicken.
  4. Wrap and serve.
