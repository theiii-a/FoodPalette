Philippines

Antonio’s

Located in Tagaytay, Antonio’s is renowned for its fine dining experience, offering a blend of European and Filipino cuisine.
The restaurant is set in a beautiful garden, providing a serene and elegant atmosphere.

Toyo Eatery

Situated in Manila, Toyo Eatery is celebrated for its modern take on traditional Filipino dishes.
Chef Jordy Navarra’s innovative approach has earned the restaurant numerous accolades, including a spot on Asia’s 50 Best Restaurants list.

Gallery by Chele

Also in Manila, Gallery by Chele offers a unique dining experience with its fusion of Filipino ingredients and global culinary techniques.
Chef Chele Gonzalez’s creative dishes highlight the rich flavors of the Philippines in a contemporary setting.

Thailand

Gaggan Anand

Located in Bangkok, Gaggan Anand’s restaurant is famous for its innovative and progressive Indian cuisine.
It has consistently ranked high on Asia’s 50 Best Restaurants list and offers a unique dining experience with a creative tasting menu.

Sühring

Also in Bangkok, Sühring is known for its modern German cuisine.
Run by twin chefs Thomas and Mathias Sühring, the restaurant combines traditional German flavors with contemporary techniques, earning it two Michelin stars.

Le Normandie

Situated at the Mandarin Oriental in Bangkok, Le Normandie offers exquisite French cuisine with stunning views of the Chao Phraya River.
It has been a culinary landmark in Bangkok for decades and holds two Michelin stars.

India

Indian Accent

Located in New Delhi, Indian Accent is renowned for its modern and inventive take on traditional Indian cuisine.
Chef Manish Mehrotra’s creative dishes have earned the restaurant numerous accolades, including a spot on Asia’s 50 Best Restaurants list.

Karavalli

Situated in Bengaluru, Karavalli offers an authentic taste of South Indian coastal cuisine.
The restaurant is celebrated for its traditional recipes and use of fresh, local ingredients, making it a favorite among food enthusiasts.

Adaa at Falaknuma Palace

Located in Hyderabad, Adaa provides a royal dining experience with its exquisite Hyderabadi cuisine.
Set in the luxurious Falaknuma Palace, the restaurant offers a unique blend of history and culinary excellence.

Japan

Sukiyabashi Jiro

Located in Tokyo, Sukiyabashi Jiro is one of the most famous sushi restaurants in the world.
Run by master sushi chef Jiro Ono, it has earned three Michelin stars and is renowned for its exceptional sushi experience.

Narisawa

Also in Tokyo, Narisawa offers innovative French-Japanese fusion cuisine.
Chef Yoshihiro Narisawa’s restaurant has been consistently ranked among the best in Asia, known for its focus on sustainability and nature-inspired dishes.

Kikunoi

Situated in Kyoto, Kikunoi is a traditional kaiseki restaurant that has been awarded three Michelin stars.
Chef Yoshihiro Murata’s meticulous attention to detail and use of seasonal ingredients make it a standout dining experience.

Italy

Osteria Francescana

Located in Modena, Osteria Francescana is helmed by Chef Massimo Bottura.
This three-Michelin-star restaurant is renowned for its innovative and artistic approach to traditional Italian cuisine. It has been consistently ranked among the best restaurants in the world.

Piazza Duomo

Situated in Alba, Piazza Duomo offers a unique dining experience with its creative and contemporary Italian dishes.
Chef Enrico Crippa’s restaurant has earned three Michelin stars and is celebrated for its use of local ingredients and avant-garde techniques.

Le Calandre

Located in Rubano, near Padua, Le Calandre is a three-Michelin-star restaurant run by Chef Massimiliano Alajmo.
Known for its modern and refined Italian cuisine, Le Calandre offers an exceptional dining experience with a focus on innovation and quality.

France

L’Arpège

Located in Paris, L’Arpège is renowned for its vegetable-focused cuisine.
Chef Alain Passard’s innovative approach has earned the restaurant three Michelin stars. The dishes highlight the freshest produce from the restaurant’s own gardens.

Mirazur

Situated in Menton, Mirazur offers stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea.

Le Bernardin

In Paris, Le Bernardin is famous for its exquisite seafood dishes.
Chef Éric Ripert’s culinary expertise has earned the restaurant three Michelin stars, making it a must-visit for seafood lovers.

Mexico

Pujol

Located in Mexico City, Pujol is helmed by Chef Enrique Olvera.
This restaurant is renowned for its modern take on traditional Mexican cuisine and has consistently been ranked among the best in the world. The tasting menu features innovative dishes that highlight the rich flavors of Mexico.

Quintonil

Also in Mexico City, Quintonil is led by Chef Jorge Vallejo.
Known for its focus on local ingredients and contemporary Mexican cuisine, Quintonil has earned a spot on the list of Latin America’s 50 Best Restaurants. The menu offers a creative and refined dining experience.

Sud 777

Situated in Mexico City, Sud 777 is celebrated for its fusion of flavors and innovative dishes.
Chef Edgar Núñez’s restaurant combines traditional Mexican ingredients with modern culinary techniques, creating a unique and memorable dining experience.

Brazil

D.O.M

Located in São Paulo, D.O.M. is helmed by Chef Alex Atala.
This restaurant is renowned for its innovative use of Brazilian ingredients and has earned two Michelin stars. It offers a unique dining experience that highlights the rich biodiversity of Brazil.

Maní

Also in São Paulo, Maní is led by Chef Helena Rizzo.
Known for its creative and contemporary Brazilian cuisine, Maní has received numerous accolades, including a spot on Latin America’s 50 Best Restaurants list. The dishes here blend Brazilian, Asian, and European influences.

Oro

Situated in Rio de Janeiro, Oro is a two-Michelin-star restaurant run by Chef Felipe Bronze.
Oro is celebrated for its modern and experimental approach to Brazilian cuisine, offering a tasting menu that showcases the best of local ingredients and culinary techniques.
